### Recommendation

1. Start with **TheDevKing** for Roblox Studio and Lua basics.
2. Use **AlvinBlox** later for advanced topics:
   - DataStores
   - Inventory systems
   - Combat systems
   - Advanced scripting

If you only pick one quickly, choose **TheDevKing**. His explanations are easier to convert into lessons for 6th–8th grade students.
