# Internet Safety

## Rule 1: Keep Personal Information Private

Never share:

- Full home address
- Phone number
- Passwords
- School name
- Parents' information

> If a stranger on the internet asks your address, should you tell them?

Answer: ❌ No

---

## Rule 2: Strong Passwords

Bad passwords:

```
123456
password
abc123
```

Better passwords:

```
TigerBlue99
MangoRocket45
```

> Never share your password, even with friends.

---

## Rule 3: Strangers Online Are Still Strangers

> If a stranger comes to your house and asks to enter, would you allow them?

No.

> The same rule applies online.

If someone they don't know messages them:

- Don't share information

- Tell parents or teachers

---

## Rule 4: Think Before You Click

Examples:

- "You won $1,000,000!"

- "Click here to get free Robux!"

- "Free iPhone!"

> If something sounds too good to be true, it probably isn't true.

---

## Rule 5: Be Kind Online

Examples:

✅ Good:

- Helping others
- Encouraging friends

❌ Bad:

- Insulting
- Bullying
- Mean comments

> Treat people online the same way you would treat them in real life.

---

## Rule 6: Ask an Adult If Unsure

If they see:

- Scary content
- Strange messages
- Something confusing

They should tell:

- Parents
- Teacher
- Trusted adult

---

## Rule 7: Not Everything Online Is True

> If someone says "Chocolate grows on trees," should we believe it immediately?

No.

> Sometimes people post wrong information online.

---

### 🛡️ Don't share personal information

### 🛡️ Never share passwords
